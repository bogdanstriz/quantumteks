RCL Express LLC

On time, Every time!

Started in 2011, RCL Express is a prominent figure in the expediting business. With over 40 straight trucks, cargo vans, sprinters and semi trucks, we have the capacity to fill any lane that you might need! Our team of dispatchers work night and day to make sure your freight gets to its destination ontime, every time. From auto parts and machinery to frozen foods and furniture, we run it all. With us, you can have the peace of mind that your critical freight will be delived intact and on time. Call us today and put your freight in good hands!

Our mission at RCL to deliver your freight on time, every time. Whether you are a broker that needs help with multiple lanes or a customer that needs an expedited shipment, our promise is that your freight will be delivered on time. We offer 24 hour location updates on all of your shipments so that you can rest easy knowing it won't be late. We value all of our customers and take pride in what we do. Call us today to see if our cost efficient shipping is for you.
